Cat Outta Here!
Logo and Game Design
Designer: Leilani Phan
Programmers: Cameron Ching, Eric Long, Jay Timbol, and Jeremy Chan

A game featuring a cat as the player. As a cat, you stole fish from your owner. Now you're running for your life to escape. This features a 2-player infinite scroller versus game. Awarded Most Creative Concept and Best Replayability.
