Wolf Play
Poster Design
Client: SJSU Department of Film, Theatre, and Dance - Will Detlefsen

Wolf Play by Hansol Jung is a theatrical play about Jeenu, a 6-year-old Korean boy who imagines he is a wolf. The client requested a minimalistic and abstract design for this poster, which promotes the production at SJSU's Hal Todd Theatre.
