Water Wood Metal Skin
Poster Redesign
Client: SJSU School of Music - Divesh Karamchandani

The concert theme, Water, Wood, Metal, Skin, is inspired by one of the featured pieces and continues the elemental theme from last semester. The client is looking for a graphic that creatively combines these four elements—water, wood, metal, and skin (drums)—into a cohesive visual.

Ticket Design

Original vs. Redesign

For this redesign of the Water Wood Metal Skin poster for SJSU School of Music, I addressed key issues from the original design. The initial poster suffered from flat colors and disconnected visual elements that failed to clearly communicate the elemental theme. The symbols lacked cohesion and didn't effectively convey their meaning to viewers. In the redesign, I emphasized the metallic quality to strengthen the connection to the elemental concept, while selecting a refined color palette that better reflects each element—water, wood, metal, and skin. Creating a more unified and visually compelling composition.
